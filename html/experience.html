EXPERIENCE

GitHub Universe takes place in the emerging creative community of Dogpatch along San
Francisco Bay's waterfront. Once the home of an industrial shipyard, the neighborhood is now
buzzing with local shops, designer studios, and nightlife that are all served by the Third Street
light rail (T Line).

TRANSPORTATION

We recommend that you use ride-sharing services, public
transportation, or bikes to arrive at Pier 70. There is no parking
available at the venue. The surrounding neighborhood has limited
parking for its residents and local businesses.

  • PUBLIC TRANSPORTATION

    The San Francisco MTA T-Line runs directly through the Dogpatch neighborhood. Take the T-Line towards
    Sunnydale + Bayshore to the 3rd St. & 20th St. stop. Pier 70 is a 10 minute walk with plenty of signs to show the
    way. If you’re taking BART, you can switch to the T-Line at Embarcadero Station.

  • RIDE-SHARING

    If you plan to travel by car, we recommend using ride-sharing services like Lyft and Uber. The address for Pier 70 is
    420 22nd Street and you should tell your driver to approach via 22nd St (and not 20th St). There will be plenty of
    signs along the way too.

  • BIKE

    Free valet bike parking is available at Pier 70.

CITY GUIDE

San Francisco is a dynamic, thriving community, and one of the
best food cities in the world. If traveling from out of town, we
recommend that you stay a few extra days to experience the magic
of Northern California.

  • WHERE TO STAY

    The majority of San Francisco’s hotels are in the Union Square and SoMa neighborhoods. These areas have easy
    access to public transportation and plenty of activities while you aren’t at Universe. Unfortunately prices at San
    Francisco hotels can be notoriously high, and we recommend Airbnb as an alternative. If you plan to stay at a hotel,
    we request that you stay at a socially responsible hotel endorsed by fairhotel.org such as:

    • Hyatt Regency San Francisco, 5 Embarcadero Center (Map)
    • Park Central Hotel, 50 Third Street (Map)
    • W San Francisco, 181 Third Street (Map)
    • Hotel Vitale, 8 Mission Street (Map)
